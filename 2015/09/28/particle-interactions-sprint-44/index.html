What we see is when you charge per device per month for services associated with the product, you run the risk of essentially consuming the margin that's available, which is to say, let's say it costs, to make the math simple, let's say it costs a dollar a year to have the device hosted, and there's ten dollars of margin, so if someone uses the product for ten years, then you've lost all of your profit.
